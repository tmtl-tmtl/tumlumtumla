About

Tùm Lum Tùm La means "All over the place" in Vietnamese.

Kevin Trieu, former cook at SPQR and Mister Jiu's, studied culinary arts at CCSF. He seeks to rediscover his heritage through re-interpreting classic Vietnamese and Chinese dishes.
