Tùm Lum Tùm La
(Thoum - Loum - Thoum - La)

Tùm Lum Tùm La is a Vietnamese phrase that is thrown around by parents to describe behavior and interests to be "all over the place" or messy.

Deciding to embrace the term, Bay-Area based KT created Tùm Lum Tùm La as a way to express themselves through different mediums.

Follow the journey as TLTA shares the fruits of their creative endeavors in and outside of the kitchen. Look forward to seeing food pop-ups, photography, zines, recipes, and more!
